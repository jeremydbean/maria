World Notes

  • One-handed logging is treated like a fast combat action.
  • Gap-building becomes mana shielding before full quitting.
  • Quests and XP reward scouting, delaying, and recovery.
  • Guild progress is opt-in and centered on encouragement.
  • Companion Mode gives the campaign a personal reason to come back.
  • Apple Watch and widget companion mockups support quick actions outside the realm.
