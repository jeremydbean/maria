Design Notes

  • One-handed logging is the primary interaction.
  • Gap-building is rewarded before full quitting.
  • Quests and XP reward tracking, delaying, and recovery.
  • Social progress is opt-in and centered on encouragement.
  • Cash Mode gives the app a personal reason to come back.
  • Apple Watch and widget mockups support quick logging away from the app.
