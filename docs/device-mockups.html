Apple Watch

Use a watchOS companion for one-tap encounter logging, urge dodges, delay timers, haptics, and a complication showing the current shield.

Widgets

Use WidgetKit for Lock Screen and Home Screen charms that display the current shield, next gate, today count, and Cash companion encouragement.

Sync

Sync iPhone, widgets, and Apple Watch through shared local app data and WatchConnectivity so watch actions update the campaign timeline.
